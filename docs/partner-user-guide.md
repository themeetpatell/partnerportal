## 1. Getting Started

### What is the Finanshels Partner Portal?

The Finanshels Partner Portal is a secure platform where approved partners can:

- **Refer clients** (leads) to Finanshels for accounting, tax, and business services
- **Submit service requests** to route clients into delivery
- **Track commissions** earned from successful referrals and conversions
- **Manage their profile**, banking details, and agreement status
- **View client activity** across the full pipeline

### System Requirements

- Modern web browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge — latest versions)
- Stable internet connection
- Email account for registration and notifications

### Accessing the Portal

- **URL**: Your partner portal URL (e.g., `https://partner.finanshels.com`)
- **Authentication**: Powered by Clerk — supports email/password, social login, and multi-factor authentication

## 2. Registration & Onboarding

### Step-by-Step Registration Flow

The registration process consists of **4 steps**:

#### Step 1: Choose Your Partner Model

You'll select one of two partnership models:

| Feature | Referral Partner | Channel Partner |
|---------|-----------------|-----------------|
| **Role** | Refer clients, earn commission | Full client management with Finanshels |
| **Initial Commission** | 30% of annual package value | 30% of annual package value |
| **Renewal Commission** | 20% on annual renewals | 20% on annual renewals |
| **Add-on Services** | 15% of add-on value | 15% of add-on value |
| **Alt Payment Plans** | 30% of first payment | 50% of first payment |
| **Approval** | Instant activation | Reviewed by team |
| **Best For** | Professionals with a network | Firms wanting to extend services |

Each model card on the registration page shows the full commission breakdown so you can make an informed decision.

#### Step 2: Company Details

Provide your basic information:

- **Company name** (required)
- **Contact name** (required)
- **Email** (pre-filled from your Clerk account)
- **Phone number** (required)

#### Step 3: Review Terms & Agreement

- Read the complete Partnership Agreement
- Review the **Commission Structure (Annexure II)** which shows:
  - Annual package commission rates
  - Add-on services rates
  - Alternative payment plan rates (varies by partner type)
- Check the agreement confirmation box
- Click "Submit application"

#### Step 4: Confirmation

- **Referral partners**: Instantly activated — you can start submitting leads right away
- **Channel partners**: Application goes to the Finanshels partnership team for review. You'll receive an email once approved.

### Post-Registration

After registration, you'll receive a confirmation email. Once approved:

1. Log in to the portal
2. Complete your profile (banking details, company info)
3. Sign your partnership agreement (if applicable)
4. Start submitting leads and service requests

## 3. Dashboard Overview

### What You'll See

The dashboard is your home base. It provides:

#### KPI Summary Cards (top row)

| Card | What It Shows |
|------|--------------|
| **Clients** | Total unique clients across leads and service requests |
| **Open Requests** | Active service requests in progress |
| **Paid Earnings** | Total settled commission value (AED) |
| **Pending Payout** | Commissions awaiting settlement |

#### Account Notices

If your account needs attention, you'll see a notice banner:

- **Pending approval**: Your account is under review
- **Rejected**: Your application was not approved (reason shown if provided)
- **Missing bank details**: Payout details incomplete — commissions accrue but cannot be settled

#### Quick Actions (top right)

- **New Lead**: Submit a referral lead
- **New Request**: Create a service delivery request
- **Commissions**: View your commission ledger

#### Recent Activity Panels

- **Recent Clients**: Latest client activity across leads and service requests
- **Recent Service Requests**: Latest delivery requests with status indicators

### Navigation Sidebar

On the left side, you'll find:

| Section | Items |
|---------|-------|
| **Revenue** | Dashboard, Submit Lead, Clients, New Service Request, Service Requests, Commissions |
| **Account** | Profile |

Click your name at the bottom of the sidebar to sign out.

## 4. Submitting Leads

### How to Submit a Lead

1. Navigate to **Submit Lead** from the sidebar (or click "New Lead" on the dashboard)
2. Fill in the lead form:

| Field | Required? | Description |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| Full Name | Yes | Client's full name |
| Email | Yes | Client's email address |
| Phone | No | Client's phone number |
| Company | No | Client's company name |
| Services Interested In | No | Select one or more services (Tax Registration, VAT Filing, Bookkeeping, Company Formation, Audit & Assurance, CFO Services) |
| Notes | No | Key context, urgency, pricing signals — anything the delivery team should know |

3. Click **Submit Lead**

### After Submission

- The lead enters the **submitted** state
- Your previous leads are shown below the form for quick reference
- The Finanshels team reviews and progresses the lead through statuses:

#### Lead Status Flow

```
Submitted → In Review → Qualified → Proposal Sent → Converted
                                                   → Rejected
```

You'll receive email notifications when a lead's status changes.

### Important Notes

- You must have an **approved** partner account to submit leads
- Duplicate leads (same email for the same partner) are prevented
- Leads with the "converted" status generate commission records automatically

## 5. Client View

### What is the Client View?

The **Clients** page provides a unified view of all your clients — combining data from both leads and service requests into a single relationship view.

### What You'll See

- **Total clients**: Combined count across referrals and delivery work
- **Lead pipeline**: Clients with active lead activity still in motion
- **Active delivery**: Clients tied to open service requests

### Client Table

Each row shows:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Client** | Company/contact name and email |
| **Lead Activity** | Latest lead status + total lead count |
| **Service Activity** | Latest service request status + service name + request count |
| **Last Activity** | Date of most recent action |

### Mobile View

On mobile, clients display as cards with the same information in a stacked layout.

## 6. Service Requests

### Creating a Service Request

1. Navigate to **New Service Request** from the sidebar
2. Fill in the form:

| Field | Required? | Description |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| Client Company | Yes | Name of the client company |
| Client Contact | Yes | Contact person's name |
| Client Email | Yes | Client's email |
| Service Type | Yes | Select from: Tax Registration, VAT Filing, Bookkeeping, Company Formation, Audit & Assurance, CFO Services |
| Description | No | Describe the requested work, timelines, context |

3. Click **Submit Request**

### Service Request Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending** | Request submitted, awaiting team review |
| **In Progress** | Finanshels team is working on the delivery |
| **Completed** | Service delivered successfully |
| **Cancelled** | Request was cancelled |

### Viewing All Requests

The **Service Requests** page shows:

- Total requests count
- Active (in-progress) vs closed counts
- Full table with client, contact, service, status, and submission date

## 7. Commissions & Payouts

### Commission Overview

The **Commissions** page shows your complete payout history:

#### Summary Cards

| Card | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Paid Earnings** | Total settled commission value |
| **Pending Payout** | Not yet settled amount |
| **Average Ticket** | Average commission size per record |
| **Total Records** | Number of entries in payout history |

### How Commissions Work

1. You submit a lead or service request
2. The lead/request progresses through the pipeline
3. When a lead converts to a deal, a commission is **automatically calculated** based on your commission model
4. Commission enters **pending** status
5. Admin approves → moves to **processing**
6. Finance settles → moves to **paid**

### Commission Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending** | Calculated, awaiting admin approval |
| **Approved** | Approved by admin, awaiting finance processing |
| **Processing** | Finance is processing the payout |
| **Paid** | Settled — funds transferred |
| **Disputed** | Under review for discrepancy |

### Payout History Table

Each row shows the commission amount (AED), status, and date.

## 8. Profile Management

### Viewing Your Profile

Navigate to **Profile** from the sidebar. Your profile page is organized into sections:

#### Header Section
- Partner type (Referral/Channel) and status badge
- Operational status (e.g., Active Partner, Yet to Activate)
- Onboarding stage (e.g., Onboarded, Yet to Onboard)

#### Profile Sections

| Section | Fields | Editable by You? |
|---------|--------|-------------------|
| **Primary Info** | Company name, contact name, email, phone, designation, DOB | Yes (except email) |
| **About & Online** | Website, LinkedIn, nationality, business size, industry, overview, address | Yes |
| **Financial & Compliance** | VAT registered, VAT number, trade license, Emirates ID/passport | Yes |
| **Banking & Payout** | Beneficiary name, bank name, bank country, IBAN, SWIFT/BIC, payment frequency | Yes |
| **CRM / Internal** | Partnership manager, appointment setter, funnel stage, activation date, etc. | No (admin-managed) |

### Editing Your Profile

1. Click the **Edit profile** button (pencil icon)
2. Modify the fields in any editable section
3. Click **Save changes**
4. A success notification confirms the update

### Fields You Cannot Edit

Some fields are managed exclusively by the Finanshels team:
- Partner type (referral/channel)
- Tier, region, country, city, channel
- Partnership manager, appointment setter
- Strategic funnel stage, activation dates
- Partnership level, agreement dates
- Commission type and rate
- Sales training status

## 9. Contract & Agreement Signing

### When You'll Need to Sign

If your partnership requires a formal agreement, you'll see a **contract signing section** on your profile page when the agreement is ready.

### Signing Process

1. An agreement will appear on your profile when the admin marks it as "sent"
2. Review the complete agreement text
3. Choose your signature method:
   - **Typed signature**: Enter your full legal name
   - **Uploaded signature**: Upload a PNG, JPG, or WEBP image (max 2 MB)
4. Enter your designation (optional)
5. Check the confirmation box
6. Click **Sign Agreement**

### After Signing

- A signed PDF is generated and stored securely
- Your contract status changes to **"Signed"**
- The signed agreement is available for download from your profile page

## 10. Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Access

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
A: On the sign-in page, click "Forgot password?" in the Clerk sign-in form. You'll receive a reset email.

**Q: My account is showing "pending". When will it be approved?**
A: The Finanshels partnership team reviews applications. Referral partners are typically approved instantly. Channel partners may take 1-3 business days.

**Q: Can I change my partner type after registration?**
A: No, partner type changes must be requested through the Finanshels team.

### Leads & Referrals

**Q: Can I edit a lead after submission?**
A: Currently, leads cannot be edited after submission. Contact the Finanshels team for corrections.

**Q: What happens when my lead gets rejected?**
A: You'll receive an email notification. The lead will show "Rejected" status in your dashboard. You can submit a new lead for the same client if circumstances change.

**Q: Is there a limit on how many leads I can submit?**
A: No limit. Submit as many qualified leads as you have.

### Commissions

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: Commissions are paid based on your payment frequency setting (monthly, quarterly, or on-request). Ensure your banking details are complete on your profile.

**Q: Why is my commission showing "disputed"?**
A: A disputed commission means there's a discrepancy being reviewed. The Finanshels finance team will resolve it and notify you.

**Q: How is my commission calculated?**
A: Based on the commission model assigned to your account (shown in your agreement). Typically:
- 30% of annual package value on initial deals
- 20% on renewals
- 15% on add-on services

### Technical

**Q: The portal isn't loading properly. What should I do?**
A: Try clearing your browser cache, using a different browser, or disabling extensions. If the issue persists, contact support.

**Q: Can I access the portal on mobile?**
A: Yes, the portal is fully responsive and works on mobile devices.
